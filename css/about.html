  About Us


Who Are We?


We are an expert in manufacturing home textile products.

Established in 2003, Shanghai Great Win Textile Co., Ltd is a professional manufacturer of home furnishing products. We are engaged in the production of stylish and comfortable throws, pillows, coverlets, bean bags, teddy bears and robes. We also make faux fur jackets, coats, vests and more.

Armed with a strong team of first rate staff members with professional capabilities, our products are of the best quality and welcomed by final customers. Our main export market is the USA market selling to TV sales buyer.

We specialize in fabric development, with over 30 years of faux fur experience. We deal with high pile, boa fur and bunny fur to use Kanecaron(Japanese Brand), Toyobo and Chinese fibers to meet different buyer’s quality requirement. We provide hundreds of newest and fashionable fur items for buyer's selection every year.

With direct fabrics supply channel, reliable quality, expertise knowledge and effective communication, we can offer one-stop business with competitive price and fast delivery.
